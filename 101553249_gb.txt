George Brown College is a college located in Toronto.
George Brown College has 3 campuses. 
The Casa Loma Campus is located at 160 kendal Ave.
This Campus is Right outside of Dupont Station.
GBC offers a variety of courses. At the CasaLoma Campus, the courses are mainly tech focused.
Courses such as programming and Computer System Technology are offered at this location.
There are many computers for use at the campus. In fact almost every room in building C has access to computers as that is where a lot of tech based labs are held.
